Pareto optimal (adj.): the property of an outcome that you can’t make anyone
better off without making someone else worse off.

strategyproof (synonym: truthful) (adj.): the property of a mechanism that
honesty is always the best policy, meaning that lying about your preferences
cannot make you better off.

mechanism (n.): a procedure for making a decision or taking an action, as a
function of what people want (i.e., of participants’ preferences).

serial dictatorship (n.): a mechanism that orders the participants, and in this
order allows each player to dictate their favorite feasible option (given the
choices made by previous players).
